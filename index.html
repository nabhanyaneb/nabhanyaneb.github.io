When I was little, I was very shy and quiet. I turned to books as my escape because it was comforting getting involed in a world of characters where I did not have to say or do anything, I could just observe. Though I have outgrown my shyness and turned into someone who is quite the opposite, I am still socially introverted, and reading is a hobby I can enjoy alone and relieve any stress I have. My favorite reads are East of Eden, A Northern Light, and The Housekeeper and the Professor.

I've been dancing since the age of 5. I've experimented with many different styles of dance, including Ballet, Lyrical, Jazz, Contemporary, Modern, Hip Hop, and even a little bit of Tap. Although I enjoy them all, my one true love is Lyrical. Lyrical taught me how to tell a story through a series of movements. From the simple reaches to the tricks and stunts, I've learned how to bring my fire to my motions. Follow me on Instagram to see clips from my performances!
