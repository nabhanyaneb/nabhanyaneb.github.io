A Little Bit About Me

I'm an undergraduate student at Rutgers University, in New Brunswick, NJ. I'm pursuing my B.S. in Computer Science. I'm graduating in December 2021, and I hope to start my full-time career as a Software Engineer. I've always been interested in problem-solving, logic, and innovation, so technology was the perfect fit for me. As the world of tech rapidly grows, I'm always looking for ways to challenge myself and expand my current skillset. I've taken courses about Artificial Intelligence, Databases, Computer Architecture, Principles of Programming Languages, Software Methodology, and Numerical Analysis Computing. I also enjoy learning from the countless resources available online and staying up to date with technical or business newsletters.

I am also working on a minor in Mathematics. Initially, I declared this minor because of its many overlaps with the field of technology, including AI, Machine Learning, and Data. I soon learned how much the subject intrigued me, and how much there is to learn. In addition to the core Calculus classes, I've taken courses about Differential Equations, Linear Optimization , and I'm currently enrolled in a course about Graph Theory. From my time at Rutgers, I've discovered realms of Mathematics that I never knew existed, and I hope to start a career that combines Mathematics and Computer Science.

In addition to my major/minor, I'm involved in a few extracurricular and volunteer activities as well. I'm a member of Women in Computer Science and Built By Girls. I was a tutor for various introductory computer science and mathematics courses at the Learning Centers, and I was a peer mentor for CS111. I'm also a dancer and a choreographer for the Rutgers Performing Dance Company. I've danced Contemporary, Jazz, Hip-Hop, Modern, Musical Theater, and Heels, and choreographed Contemporary and Jazz.

Outside of my academic and professional career, I have a passion for the arts. I listen to a lot of music in my free time and I'm always looking for new music to explore. My other hobbies include reading, drawing, painting, and dancing. Lastly, my Myers-Briggs personality type is INTJ!
